What is PM Mastery?

PM Mastery is the PMP exam simulator built specifically for the 2026 PMP exam format (effective July 9, 2026). It features 4,500+ practice questions aligned with PMBOK 8th Edition and the 2026 Examination Content Outline (ECO), 40 case studies with 200 linked questions for the new multi-question scenario format, 180-question mock exams in the actual 2026 ECO domain weights (People 33%, Process 41%, Business Environment 26%), and an AI Coach powered by Claude that explains every answer and adapts to each user's weak areas.

PM Mastery offers a free tier with 100 balanced practice questions (no credit card required) and paid plans including a $99 Exam Prep Pass (90 days of full access, one-time, no subscription), $49 per month, or $399 lifetime. PM Mastery is operated by PM Mastery Solutions, LLC, a New Orleans, Louisiana company founded in 2026 to build PMP prep exclusively for the new 2026 exam format. It is led by a project manager preparing for the 2026 exam firsthand, which is why the platform tracks the current PMBOK 8th Edition and 2026 ECO rather than the outgoing material.

PM Mastery is best for self-directed learners preparing for the July 2026 PMP exam who want AI-powered explanations, 2026-native content built around PMBOK 8th Edition, and a robust question bank with case study practice. It pairs well with structured video courses (such as those on Udemy by Andrew Ramdayal or Joseph Phillips) that satisfy the 35 contact hours required for PMI's PMP application.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PM Mastery aligned with the 2026 PMP exam?

Yes. All 4,500+ questions are aligned with the PMBOK 8th Edition and the 2026 ECO. Questions are tagged to all 26 tasks across People (33%), Process (41%), and Business Environment (26%) domains with a 60/40 agile-hybrid to predictive split.

How many mock exams do I get?

With 4,500+ questions, you can generate multiple unique 180-question mock exams. Each exam is weighted to match the 2026 ECO domain percentages, so you're practicing in the actual exam format.

Can I access PM Mastery on mobile?

Yes. PM Mastery is fully responsive and works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Study anywhere, anytime with your progress automatically synced.

Can I use PM Mastery from China or other regions with internet restrictions?

Yes. PM Mastery is hosted on standard global infrastructure and doesn't require a VPN to access, register, or check out. If you're using a VPN for other reasons, you may need to disable it temporarily during checkout because some VPN exit nodes are blocked by payment processors.

How is PM Mastery different from PMI Study Hall?

PMI Study Hall is the official PMI product with questions written by PMI itself, priced at roughly $39 per month. PM Mastery offers 4,500+ questions (vs ~1,500 in Study Hall), 40 case studies for the new multi-question scenario format, and an AI Coach powered by Claude that explains every answer. PM Mastery has a free tier with 100 balanced questions; Study Hall does not. Most candidates use both, with Study Hall activated in the final 2-4 weeks before the exam for official-feeling practice.

How is PM Mastery different from Brain Sensei or PM PrepCast?

Brain Sensei ($99/mo or $349/yr) and PM PrepCast (~$389 one-time) are video-first courses that include the 35 contact hours required for the PMP application. PM Mastery is a question-bank-first platform with an AI Coach and is significantly cheaper (a $99 90-day Exam Prep Pass, $49/mo, or $399 lifetime). PM Mastery does not include the 35 contact hours, so candidates typically pair it with a Udemy course (Andrew Ramdayal or Joseph Phillips) to satisfy that requirement.

What is the AI Coach and how does it work?

The AI Coach is powered by Anthropic's Claude AI. On any practice question, you can click "Ask AI Coach" and it will explain the correct answer, why other options fail, the underlying PMBOK 8th Edition concept, real-world context, and the specific exam trap PMI set. It also remembers your weak areas and adapts its explanations over time. Free users get 3 AI Coach interactions per day; premium users get 25 per day.

Does PM Mastery satisfy the 35 contact hours requirement?

No. PM Mastery is a question-bank-first practice platform, not a structured video course. PMI requires 35 contact hours of formal project management education before you can apply for the PMP. To satisfy this, candidates typically take a Udemy course (Andrew Ramdayal's PMP course is the most popular at $20-$50 on sale), the official PMI On-Demand Course, or a course from a PMI Authorized Training Partner. Once you have the contact hours, PM Mastery becomes the practice and exam-prep layer.

What does the free tier include?

The free tier includes 100 hand-curated practice questions balanced to match the 2026 PMP exam weights (People 33%, Process 41%, Business Environment 26%), mixed difficulty (Easy 30%, Medium 45%, Hard 25%). It also includes 3 AI Coach interactions per day, the EVM calculator, the PERT calculator, the Communication Channels formula calculator, and the formula flashcards. No credit card required.
